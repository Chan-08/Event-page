About B S Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and Technology

(Formerly known as Crescent Engineering college) was founded by Mr. B. S. Abdur Rahman in 1984.

It is among one of the top universities in the country and it also is recognized for its “Sustainable Development Growth” initiatives. Our esteemed institution serves as an intellectual beacon, challenging conventional paradigms while igniting a fervent pursuit of knowledge.

Its unparalleled pedagogical approach fosters a culture of self-improvement and healthy competition among students and scholars alike. Beyond its commitment to academic excellence, the verdant campus and meticulously curated student life offerings are tailored to maximize the holistic educational experience.

Through a diverse range of educational initiatives and bespoke clubs designed to nurture personal and professional growth, we cultivate a sense of belonging that transcends mere academic pursuit, establishing a lifelong home for our community.

About the Crescent Student Council

The CRESCENT STUDENT COUNCIL is an executive body under the Office of the Student Affairs composed of students to address issues and concerns affecting the student body.

It also works on the development of the students by conducting various events including seminars, workshops, etc.

They serve as a liaison between students, faculty, and administration, voicing student opinions and concerns to the university's decision-makers.

In brief, Crescent Student Council serves as a platform for student leadership, engagement, and representation within the campus community, working to enhance the overall student experience and contribute to the academic and social development of the institution.
