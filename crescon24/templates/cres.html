About CresCon'24

Crescon24, taking place on April 15th, 16th, and 17th, offers a dynamic three-day event fostering a symbiotic relationship between speakers and students. Through panel discussions, workshops, and student-led activities, it facilitates a vibrant exchange of knowledge and ideas. With 60 stalls showcasing innovations and offering branded promotions, Crescon24 provides a platform for connection, collaboration, and enrichment for all involved.

  • Theme

    Crescon'24 - Explore, Excel, and Empower, the pioneer national-level convention dedicated to the pursuit of a sustainable future.

  • Objective

    It stands as a testament to the innovative spirit of our university, aiming to pave the way for transformative change.

  • Participation

    Bringing together thought leaders, experts, professionals, students, and enthusiasts from diverse fields under one roof.

Purpose of the event

The platform features diverse events like speeches, panel discussions, workshops, and seminars by accomplished speakers and guests in various fields and art forms. It celebrates excellence across science, art, business, and humanities, showcasing the richness and versatility of human achievement.

  • Enlightening Minds

    The primary objective of our informative and entertaining event is to enlighten the students at our university and beyond. To educate them about various factors required to develop themselves and our society.

  • Workshops

    Workshops can equip students with hands-on skills, enhancing their capabilities in areas such as communication, problem-solving, or technical expertise. Hearing from experts in various fields during panel discussions can expose students to diverse perspectives and insights.

  • CC-24 EXPO

    Cc24 Expo..Explore the latest cool stuff at the Crescon'24 Expo. A showcase of cutting-edge technologies, groundbreaking research, and revolutionary ideas of advancement driving the technical society and contributing to further technological innovations.

Target of the event

Students gain exposure to successful personalities, enhancing their knowledge across diverse fields. This event fosters holistic development by addressing academic, professional, cultural, and personal aspects. It cultivates an environment promoting learning, collaboration, and excellence in various dimensions.

Career Guidance – Seminar

A tailored career guidance seminar for university students offers benefits beyond the classroom. It provides clarity on career goals, helping align skills and passions with viable options. Seminars also outline skill development roadmaps, empowering students to acquire competencies through coursework and internships. Networking opportunities with industry professionals and alumni enhance prospects for mentorship, internships, and job placements. For graduating students, these seminars aid post-graduation planning, contributing to a smoother transition into the professional realm. Overall, they serve as comprehensive support systems, empowering students for success beyond university.
