Workshops

  • Design Storm

    "Design Storm: Design Workshop for Beginners" is a comprehensive workshop that introduces participants to the fundamentals of graphic design using Canva. Led by Zeena Qaaida, Co-Founder of CDMT and a seasoned freelance designer, this workshop promises an immersive learning experience. Participants will learn essential design principles, explore Canva's features, and engage in hands-on exercises to create captivating graphics. Whether you're a student, professional, or entrepreneur, this workshop will equip you with practical skills to enhance your design capabilities. Join us at CresCon '24 and unlock your creativity!

  • SustainaVerse

    "SustainaVerse: Coding a Sustainable Universe" Welcome to our Sustainable Development Codeathon! This event is a groundbreaking initiative where participants collaborate to develop software solutions tackling pressing sustainability challenges. Our codeathon is a platform for innovation. Join us to harness diverse perspectives and expertise in addressing global issues. Whether you're a seasoned developer or passionate about sustainability, this is your chance to make a difference. Let's come together to create impactful solutions and pave the way for a more sustainable future.

  • FutureScape

    "FutureScape: Navigating Gen AI - A Seminar on Inclusive AI for Next-Gen Empowerment" Come explore the fascinating world of AI catered to Gen Z as well as future generations at our tech seminar on Gen AI. Explore a new generation of AI systems made to appeal people from diverse backgrounds. With cutting-edge technology that is accessible, inclusive, and intuitive, Gen AI promises to empower people through everything from industry revolutions to daily productivity enhancements. Take advantage of this chance to learn how Gen AI is influencing the future and how you can use it to advance your career and personal life.
